Regulatory Compliance

ArmsDealer operates in full compliance with all applicable federal and state firearms regulations. Below is a summary of our compliance framework.

ATF

Federal Firearms License

ArmsDealer holds a valid Type 01 FFL (Dealer in Firearms) issued by the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives. License Number: 12-AB-12345. All transactions comply with 18 U.S.C. Chapter 44 and 27 CFR Part 478.

NICS

Background Checks

All qualifying purchases are subject to a National Instant Criminal Background Check System (NICS) check initiated at the point of transfer. ArmsDealer does not complete any transfer to a buyer whose NICS check returns a denial.

EAR / ITAR

Export Controls

International orders are processed in compliance with the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) and, where applicable, the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR). All exports are subject to Department of Commerce and/or State licensing where required.

STATE

State-Level Restrictions

We actively maintain a database of state-specific restrictions including assault weapons bans, magazine capacity limits, suppressor prohibitions, and waiting period requirements. Products unavailable in your state are hidden at checkout. Buyers are still responsible for verifying local compliance.

NFA

National Firearms Act Items

NFA-regulated items (suppressors, short-barreled rifles, machine guns, destructive devices, AOWs) are sold in compliance with 26 U.S.C. § 5801 et seq. Buyers must submit ATF Form 4 and the applicable tax stamp fee. ArmsDealer holds the item until ATF approval is received.

AML

Anti-Money Laundering

ArmsDealer maintains an AML compliance program consistent with FinCEN guidance for firearms dealers. Transactions above reporting thresholds trigger CTR filings. Suspicious activity is reported via SAR as required.

Compliance inquiries: compliance@armsdealer.com
