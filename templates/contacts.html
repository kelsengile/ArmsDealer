[ SECTION 03 ]

Shipping Policy

01

Domestic Shipping (USA)

Standard ground: 3–7 business days. Priority: 1–3 business days. Expedited overnight available for non-NFA items under $5,000. All firearms ship Adult Signature Required.

02

International Shipping

Available to 48 countries. All international shipments include full export documentation, EEI filing, and carrier insurance up to declared value. Transit times: 7–21 business days.

03

Carrier Partners

We use FedEx, UPS, and USPS depending on item type and destination. Firearms are shipped only via approved carriers compliant with 18 U.S.C. § 922(e).

04

Shipping Rates

Calculated at checkout based on weight, destination, and carrier selection. Orders over $2,500 qualify for complimentary insured ground shipping within the contiguous US.

05

FFL Transfer

All handguns and long guns require transfer through a licensed FFL dealer in your state. We ship directly to your chosen FFL dealer; buyer completes the transfer in-store.

06

Restricted Items

NFA items require additional paperwork and a 6–12 month ATF approval window before shipment. Certain items cannot be shipped to CA, NY, MA, MD, or IL — restrictions noted on each product listing.

[ SECTION 04 ]

Returns Policy

Due to the regulated nature of firearms commerce, our returns process has strict requirements. Please read carefully before initiating a return.

ITEM CATEGORY RETURN WINDOW CONDITION REQUIRED REFUND TYPE
Firearms (unfired) 7 days Original packaging, no transfer completed Full refund
Accessories / Optics 30 days Unused, original packaging Full refund or store credit
Ammunition Non-returnable — Store credit (defective only)
NFA Items Non-returnable — Contact compliance team
Clothing / Gear 30 days Unworn, tags attached Full refund or exchange

To initiate a return, contact support@armsdealer.com with your order number and reason. Unauthorized returns will be refused at our dock. Return shipping costs are the buyer's responsibility unless the item arrived defective or incorrect.

[ SECTION 05 ]

Warranty

ArmsDealer honors all manufacturer warranties on new products. We also offer our own ArmsDealer Assurance program for additional coverage.

MANUFACTURER

OEM Warranty

All new firearms and accessories sold through ArmsDealer carry the full manufacturer's warranty. Warranty claims are submitted directly to the manufacturer; we provide all necessary purchase documentation and act as liaison where needed.

Coverage period varies by manufacturer — typically 1 year to lifetime on receivers.

ARMSDEALER ASSURANCE

Extended Coverage

Our optional Assurance plan extends coverage for an additional 2 years beyond the manufacturer warranty, covering mechanical failures not caused by user damage or unauthorized modification.

Available at checkout for eligible items. Priced from 4% of item value.

EXCLUSIONS

What's Not Covered

  • Damage from improper use or modification
  • Normal wear & tear (springs, grips, barrel wear)
  • Cosmetic damage not affecting function
  • Items purchased as surplus or used-graded
  • Damage from corrosive / non-spec ammunition

For warranty claims, email support@armsdealer.com with your order number, a description of the defect, and photos where applicable. Our team will respond within 2 business days.
