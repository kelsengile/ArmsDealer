Who We Are

ArmsDealer is a federally licensed firearms and tactical equipment marketplace built for professionals, enthusiasts, and collectors who demand integrity and precision at every step of the transaction.

We operate as a fully regulated FFL dealer, maintaining strict compliance with all applicable federal, state, and local laws. Every product listed on our platform undergoes verification before it ever reaches a buyer's hands.

Our digital storefront bridges the gap between traditional gun-shop trust and modern e-commerce convenience — giving qualified buyers access to a verified, curated inventory without sacrificing safety or accountability.

Our Story

2025

Foundation

ArmsDealer was founded by a group of former military servicemen and licensed gunsmiths who saw a gap in the market: reputable, digital-first firearms commerce with zero tolerance for grey-market transactions.

Q2 2025

Platform Launch

Platform beta went live with an initial catalog of 400 SKUs across rifles, handguns, optics, and tactical gear. First 500 orders fulfilled within the first 30 days.

Q4 2025

Expansion

Partnerships established with 14 verified manufacturers. Catalog grew to 3,400+ SKUs. International shipping lanes opened to 48 countries with full customs documentation support.

2026

Today

Serving over 12,000 verified buyers globally. Continuously investing in compliance infrastructure, secure logistics, and platform improvements. The mission hasn't changed: trusted arms commerce, built right.
