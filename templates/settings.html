Frequently Asked Questions
Answers to the most common issues around account, payments, and listings.
▶
How do I create a product listing?▼
Navigate to your Dashboard → Inventory → Add New Product. Fill in the product name, category, price, description, and upload images. Products marked Authorized are visible to all users; unmarked listings are guest-restricted.
How does the escrow / payment system work?▼
Funds from a buyer are held in escrow until the seller marks the order as shipped and the buyer confirms receipt, or until the order status moves to Delivered. Disputes can be raised through the Contact Support form.
Why is my account showing as restricted?▼
Restricted accounts have limited listing visibility. This happens when compliance verification is incomplete or when an admin has flagged the account for review. Submit a support ticket below for assistance.
How do I change my currency or language?▼
Go to Settings → Language & Region. You can set your display language, timezone, and preferred currency. Changes take effect immediately and are saved per session.
How do I enable two-factor authentication?▼
Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Two-Factor Authentication and toggle it on. You will need an authenticator app (e.g. Google Authenticator or Authy). Scan the QR/secret key and enter the 6-digit code to confirm.
Can I recover a deleted account?▼
No. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All data including listings, orders, and messages are permanently erased. If you need a break, use Deactivate Account instead — you can reactivate any time.
How long does support take to respond?▼
Support tickets are reviewed by the admin team during operating hours. You will receive a reply to your registered email address within 24–48 hours. For urgent issues, mark your ticket as Technical Problem.
